EMPOWERING PEOPLE THROUGH INFORMATION

Benefits Ninja is forcing a discussion towards a more balanced environment, that would work for various individuals. We believe that by making this data public, everyone benefits. Employers gain insight into what employees really want in a workplace, and Employees are more likely to make an informed choice that is sustainable in the long term

Data has proven that there is huge disparity in the basic benefits offered to Employees across the US. By putting this out in the public with real numbers backing them, we believe that the Government is forced to pay attention as well.
